South African Malva Pudding

Description

Malva Pudding is a sweet and sticky dessert of South African origin, featuring a caramelized exterior and a spongy, moist interior, often served with a creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Apricot jam
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Vinegar
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Beat sugar and eggs until fluffy, then blend in apricot jam.
  2. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt together.
  3. Melt butter and mix with vinegar and milk.
  4. Combine all ingredients and pour into a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 350°F (175°C) until golden brown.
  6. Pour over a buttery cream sauce before serving.
