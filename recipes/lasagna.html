Lasagna

Description

Cheesy Italian Lasagna with Tomato Sauce is a simple, beginner-friendly dish.

It layers lasagna noodles with tomato sauce and a mix of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses, baked until golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese,shredded
  • Parmesan cheese,grated
  • Egg (for the ricotta mixture)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg and a pinch of salt.
  5. Spread the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  6. Add a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layering of sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella until the dish is full.
  8. Top with a final layer of mozzarella and some grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for another 20 minutes until the cheese is golden.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
